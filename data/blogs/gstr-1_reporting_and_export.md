## Understanding GSTR-1 Reporting
GSTR-1 is a monthly or quarterly return that businesses must file with the GST authorities, detailing all outward supplies of goods and services. The report includes information such as invoice numbers, dates, and values, as well as the GST rates applicable to each supply. The deadline for filing GSTR-1 is typically the 10th of the following month for monthly filers and the 10th of the month following the quarter for quarterly filers.
